Techo-Cultural Fest

For the past decennium, PDPM IIITDM, Jabalpur has set higher and higher benchmarks in multifarious domains of culture, providing its participants a platform to promulgate their talent and explore their passion in the field of Drama, Music, Arts, Literature and Dance through its annual cultural festival "TARANG". TARANG has witnessed numerous enthralling performances by top-notch artists. Comedy Night, Theatre Night, Fashion Show and Prof Night are the most sparkling, scintillating and happening evenings of TARANG. Previously these major nights have been decorated by performances from "AGNEE", "VH-1Supersonic “UNDERGROUND AUTHORITY” , “JAL” , ”BANDISH“ , ”HARD KAUR” , ”ARYANS” , ”DJ Sumit” (Channel V Fame) , ”SHILPA RAO” , ”KUMAR VISHWAS” , ”RAHET INDORI” , ”EHSAAN QURESHI” AND ”SUNBURN”. It attracted primarily the students and young professionals of Jabalpur .The “Fashion Show- VOGUE Night”, “AAVEG- The Rock Band Competition”, “EDM NITE”, “COMEDY NIGHTS” ,”Theatre Night” and “Dance Beat” makes the shows cover most of the dimensions of presentable art.The institute has also been helping the young technocrats of India showcase their technical might and innovative abstractions through its annual technical festival ABHIKALPAN . Abhikalpan features a wide array of workshops pertaining to different fields of interests. Among the highly rated ones are the workshops on Graphic Design, Raspberry-pi, Bluebotics , Android ,Ethical Hacking(conducted by EISystems), Motion Sensing, Calligraphy(conducted by Achyut Pallav). In addition to the workshops, technical competitive events are what entice the technical paraphernalia the most. Amongst the most crowd gathering events are Robowar, Debugging, Race Pulse, Mechbot, Aqua Robo di Fifa, Web Designing, The GD competition and many more.

Workshop Organised by Women Cell

International Women Day is celebrated all across the world on 8th march. On this occasion, Women cell of PDPM IIITDMJ organized a “Workshop on Self-Defense” for the female students of the institute. The workshop was conducted by Mr. Amit Das who is black belt and weapon trainer of security guards. In the workshop, Mr. Das introduced the basics of self-defense, which were practical and quite easy to apply. He discussed about the psychological attacks and defence towards them.

Advanced Data Mining Algorithms and their Scalability for Big Data

This course aims to introduce advanced data mining algorithms to students who have some knowledge about basic data mining algorithms. Advanced classification and clustering algorithms are presented and analyzed. The data type of graphs is introduced and various mining tasks with graph data are presented and analyzed. Scalability of some data mining algorithms and some graph processing algorithms using the MapReduce paradigm for Hadoop environments is presented and analyzed..
